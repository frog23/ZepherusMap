Zepherus Map

An unofficial map of the videos created by Zepherus.
See his Youtube channel for the complete list of videos (including those that are not geo-referenceable) or you can look up the coordinates for individual videos in the Review Interface.


You can contribute to this map over at https://github.com/frog23/ZepherusMap. Other feedback, questions and suggestions can be send to maps@frog23.net.
